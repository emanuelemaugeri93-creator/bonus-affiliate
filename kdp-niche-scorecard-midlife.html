📖 Criteria & Strategy Guide
🔥

Demand (30 pts)

Largest weight. No buyers = no sales, regardless of quality.

  • Publisher Rocket: exact monthly search volume
  • Amazon autocomplete speed as a proxy signal
  • Google Trends: 12-month direction check
  • Evergreen = animals, floral, mandalas
Ref: KdpEasy formula · Publisher Rocket methodology
⚔️

Competition (25 pts)

Low competition = faster organic ranking and lower ad spend.

  • Under 10,000 Books results = entereable (KdpEasy 2025)
  • Under 50 reviews on page 1 = weak barrier
  • Generic/ugly covers = your pro design wins immediately
Ref: LowContentProfits Dec 2025 · KdpEasy analysis
💰

Profitability (25 pts)

Price at $9.99+ to get 60% royalty (mandatory since June 2025 KDP update).

  • BSR under 100k = actively selling right now
  • Net per sale at $9.99 ≈ $3.84 after print cost
  • 130 sales/mo at $9.99 = ~$500 net per book
Ref: KDP royalty update Jun 2025 · Automateed analysis
🛠️

Feasibility (20 pts)

Can you launch a competitive book in 1–2 weeks with AI tools?

  • MidJourney / DALL-E: coloring interiors in hours
  • Canva KDP templates: cover in 1–2 hours
  • High gap = obvious angle competitors missed
Ref: AI publishing workflows 2025 · Book Bolt data
🎯

Score Thresholds

  • 80–100 🟢 GO — Strong signal. Launch now.
  • 65–79 🟡 Maybe — Good but needs sub-niche pivot.
  • 45–64 🟠 Risky — Only if you have an unfair edge.
  • Under 45 🔴 Skip — Better ROI elsewhere.
🚀

Pro Tips (Data-Backed)

  • Score 5–8 niches before committing to one
  • High BSR demand + under 50 reviews = goldmine combo
  • Always price at $9.99 minimum for 60% royalty
  • Micro-niches under 3k results = best entry in 2026
  • Combine 2 themes: "cats + cottagecore" = near-zero comp.
